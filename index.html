Privacy by design

Not a policy. An
architecture.

Every privacy protection in LUMARA is enforced at the technical level — not written into terms of service.

📱

Data lives on your device

Your journal, history, and personal context are stored locally. Not on our servers. Not synced to the cloud by default. On your phone.

✂️

Scrubbed before the cloud

When context does reach the model, sensitive identifying details are stripped first. The model gets signal, not your raw data.

🔐

Encrypted at rest

Everything stored on your device is encrypted. If your phone is compromised, your LUMARA data isn't readable.

🚫

Never used for training

Your conversations and journal are yours. They are not used to improve the model, not shared with third parties, not analyzed for advertising.
